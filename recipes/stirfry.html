Basic Meat Sandwich

  • Brown Rice
  • Chicken Breasts
  • Bell peppers
  • Onions
  • Mushrooms
  • Tomatoes
  • Spices
  1. Cook a cup of dry brown rice
  2. Cook chicken to liking
  3. Fry veggies in olive oil
  4. Add rice to veggies in the pan
  5. And spices, soy sauce, chicken and tomatoes, and mix evenly
  6. Serve in bowl or on plate, with spoon or fork
