Basic Meat Sandwich

  • 2 slices of yoru bread of choice
  • Roast Beef slices
  • Mustard, Mayo, hot sauce
  • Mayonase
  • Sriracha sauce
  • Cheese
  1. Lay out slices of bread
  2. Spread mayonase over bread, and squirt out some mustard and hot sauce
  3. Put meat on top of one of the prepared slices of bread
  4. Add 2 slices of block cheese on top of meat and put on top slice of bread
